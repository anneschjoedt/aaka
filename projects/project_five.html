About

Explainer video

I created an explainer video as part of the course Principle of Animation at University of Sydney.

Challenge

The video should explain a company's product or service in no more or less than a minute.

Process

I decided to create an explainer video for audio guides offered at museums, to focus attention on the use and benefits of audio guides in the museum space. The video was intended to communicate in a witty and informal way to accommodate guests who feel alienated in the museum space.

Result

All the visual material used in the video was drawn in Adobe Illustrator and animated in Adobe After Effects.
