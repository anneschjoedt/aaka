O / Day Fest

In collaboration with microbrewery ÅBEN I developed a webpage for ticket purchase of festival experiences and merchandise.

Challenge

ÅBEN is a danish microbrewery founded in 2017 and currently located in Kolding. ÅBEN was named Brewery of The Year in 2019, and has since taken over a beer bar in Roskilde, opened an ÅBEN beer bar in Aarhus and launched a Summer Pop Up in the heart of Copenhagen. In August 11 -13 2022 they host a new festival experience in Copenhagen. They needed an online shopping experience in line with their already existing homepage o-dayfest.dk.

Process

In close collaboration with ÅBEN and Ticketmaster I developed the webpage from the Figma prototype provided from the graphic design team at ÅBEN and handed over the webpage to Ticketmaster who was in charge of getting the website live.

Result

The webpage was created in Visual Studio Code with the use of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Flexbox.
