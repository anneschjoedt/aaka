T-shirt design

After having won the design competition held by Makwerk, my design was sold as print on posters and t-shirts on dimsstudio.com and in the shop in Århus.

Challenge

Dimsstudio and Makwerk held a design competition in which designers could contribute with a design. The 10 best designs were picked by Makwerk and customers could then vote for the best 3 designs, which would be printed on t-shirts and sold.

Process

I started out by drawing my Frida Kahlo illustration by hand with ink on paper. The illustration were then scanned and edited in Photoshop.

Result

The design won the competition together with two other designs and were printed on t-shirts. The design ended up being highly in demand and were later printed on posters as well.
