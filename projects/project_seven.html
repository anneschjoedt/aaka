AnnóAnno

In collaboration with AnnóAnno - a personal styling universe for curvy women - I designed a new company website and a personal webshop experience.

Challenge

AnnóAnno's mission is to offer a universe of personal inspiration and individual guidance, where curvy woman can buy unique and personally curated clothes to embrace and express themselves truly. They needed an updated website which clearly conveyed their mission and value propositions. A few month later I helped design a personal webshop experience and an updated mypage (user profile). The webshop experience should complement their styling concept and intuitivly feel personal to each customer.

Process

In close collaboration with the team at AnnóAnno I designed both website and webshop in Figma. The webshop was designed based on a component library called PrimeBlocks as the design was developed in Vue. The design was handed over to a graphic designer which added the finishing touch.

Result

The website was developed in Webflow by AnnóAnno's own developing team and is now live, while the webshop is scheduled to launch in April.
