About

Maths Camp

My thesis buddy and I designed, developed and tested a personalised math learning app targeted primary school students in close collaboration with Halstead Preparatory School for Girls and Rygaards International School.

Challenge

In collaboration with Halstead Preparatory School for Girls and associate professor in computer science Mircea Lungu we discovered the need for a math learning app that would adapt to each student's math level while they were using it. The app should motivate less confident students in their practice by incoporating gamification elements such as mascots, reward and badge collections.

Process

To accomplish our goal, we developed a design based on feedback from actual users. The design process included everything from UI and UX design, datamodelling, prototype and final product development. Our data-driven process included qualitative user interviews and usability tests. We developed the prototype in Figma and the app in React with a backend in Back4app.

Result

The app, Maths Camp, improved the users experience in practicing math problems by optimising their learning experience and keeping them motivated along the way. We worked in a data-driven way and collected data from actual users through interviews, surveys and tools such as Hotjar and turned the insights into requirement specifications. Our thesis recieved the highest grade possible and a new team of developers are currently continuing working on the app.
